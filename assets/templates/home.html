Features

Our IDS is designed with advanced functionalities to secure networks, detect threats, and provide quick responses.

  • Traffic Analysis

  • Attack Detection

  • Automation

  • Reporting

Deep Packet & Traffic Inspection

IDS examines every packet passing through the network, analyzing source, destination, and behavior to identify risks.

  • Identifies abnormal bandwidth usage.
  • Detects unusual port scanning activity.
  • Monitors data packets for malicious patterns.

Malware & Attack Identification

The system recognizes different types of attacks such as DDoS, SQL injection, and brute force attempts with high accuracy.

  • Detects common and advanced cyberattacks.
  • Uses signature-based and anomaly-based methods.
  • Blocks repeated malicious attempts.

Automated Security Responses

IDS can automatically trigger alerts, block suspicious IP addresses, and restrict unauthorized access without manual action.

  • Auto-blocks blacklisted IPs.
  • Integrates with firewalls for active defense.
  • Reduces human effort in threat response.

Detailed Alerts & Reports

IDS provides detailed logs and reports of detected threats to help administrators analyze incidents and improve security.

  • Real-time alerts via email or dashboard.
  • Generates daily/weekly threat reports.
  • Helps in forensic analysis after an incident.

How It Work

Our IDS follows a simple 4-step process to monitor and detect malicious activities in real-time:

1

Capture Network Traffic

Continuously monitor incoming and outgoing packets across the network.

2

Analyze with Detection Engine

Inspect traffic using signature and anomaly-based methods to detect threats.

3

Identify Suspicious Activities

Spot unusual patterns, unauthorized access, or malicious behaviors.

4

Alert Admin / Log Incident

Notify security teams and store logs for forensic investigation.
